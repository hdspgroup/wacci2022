Tourist recommendations

Bucaramanga is known as one of the most visited cities in Colombia, and it also known as the pretty city and the city of the parks. Bucaramanga has a kindly wether around 27 celcius grades all the year. In this section you can find what you can do in Bucaramanga. 


Visit the parks


As mentioned above, Bucaramanga has many parks that respect nature and the ecosystem, so one of the activities you can do is walk through some of its parks and get to know them. Here are some of the most popular parks you should visit:
San Pio

Located in Commune 12, it is one of the biggest park of the city. It counts with 233 trees. It has an emblematic art piece of the "Standing nude girl" sculpted by Botero. This is an ideal place to share in company of your lovely family and friends. Around this park, you can find several restaurants, accessible transportation, and shopping areas.

La flora

La flora is an ecological park known as the lung of Bucaramanga by many Bumangeses. It counts with wide and long trails full of nature. This site is full of flora and fauna and many green spaces where there are zones for exercising. This site is ideal for a picnic or simply to enjoy the fresh air surrounded by nature.

We recommend you to visit this website if you want to know about more parks of Bucaramanga.

Visit historical and cultural places


Bucaramanga emerges as an urban center, ending the colonial period. During that period (1600), several leaders met to sign an act of completion of the church and sacristy, where this information was later distributed with limits very similar to the city's current limits. If you want more information, please visit Origin of Bucaramanga. From this history, some impressive stories and buildings exist to this day, so we recommend you visit:
Eastern Cultural Center

Located in the center of Bucaramanga, it was declared a National Monument in 1995. Founded by San Pedro Claver and Nuestra Señora del Pilar, it is a site used for temporary exhibitions and contains the history of Bucaramanga in its architecture. In this link you can see more about our heritage, five videos that teach you about this building.

La mesa de los santos

At La mesa de los Santos, you can see the famous Chicamocha Canyon with its beautiful views. Likewise, it also contains many activities related to the rich culture of Santander, where you can visit a square that performs live presentations surrounded by numerous restaurants that offer a wide variety of typical Santanderean dishes.

You can find more beautiful and historic places of Bucaramanga here.

Visit and enjoy La mesa de los Santos

We strongly recommend you visit the Mesa de los Santos, a beautiful place full of nature and the purest Santanderean customs. There you can do a lot of activities, among which we recommend the following:
Visit El mercado campesino de acuarela
The residents of the municipality market their products in the watercolor farmers market (mercado campesino de Acuarela), also located on La mesa de los Santos, where you can enjoy both the dishes they offer and their crafts and Santanderean culture.

Visit Centro Cultural Mi Colombia Querida
Less than 2 km from the Mercado Campesino is a replica of a "guane town" with many family development activities in an open space. At Mi Colombia we recommend you visit the open-air butterfly garden and enjoy mountain biking.
Drink the best coffee of Colombia
It was not mentioned before, but one of the best activities that can be done on this site is to visit La hacienda El Roble, where coffee experts consider the best organic coffee in the world is grown there. In this activity, you can do a coffee tasting where you can learn a little more about its history in Colombia and do various tastings to check for yourself the quality of its flavor.
The coffee tour costs only $8.55 and consists of visiting the museum of the 72 varieties of coffee and the Beneficio plant. There will be a tasting of exotic types of coffee, followed by a tour of the main house, and finally, you will be able to receive a specialized conference optionally.
You can visit the official store to take the coffee you liked the most and different products related to coffee tasting.
