BUCARAMANGA

Bucaramanga is the capital of the department of Santander in the north of Colombia. It is known for its many parks, including the Water Park. Bucaramanga is connected to other cities in the country by road; for air transport it has the Palonegro International Airport. The city has been declared by the World Bank in its study of "competitive cities for studies and growth" as one of the most prosperous cities in Latin America.
